The AI Workflow

1

Data Ingestion

The system securely connects to Open Banking APIs to fetch real-time transaction data, user consent logs, and account metadata. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256.

2

Deep Analysis

Our Neural Network (Transformer Model) processes the data stream. It looks for behavioral patterns, anomaly spikes, and cross-references global fraud databases in under 40ms.

3

Action & Insight

Based on the risk score, the system automatically triggers actions: blocking suspicious transactions, flagging for review, or approving instantly. Insights are pushed to the dashboard.
